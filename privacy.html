Privacy Policy

App Name: Fibres - Progress Photos

Last Updated: April 2026

Your privacy is our priority. Fibres - Progress Photos (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is designed with a "Local First" approach, meaning your data stays under your control.

1. Photos and Image Data

All photos added to Fibres remain exclusively on your device. We do not maintain any cloud storage or servers for your images. Your progress photos are stored within the App's local sandbox and are not accessible by other applications or uploaded to any third-party services.

2. Security and Biometrics

The App offers optional security features including Face ID and a custom 4-digit PIN.

  • Face ID: When enabled, the App uses Apple’s LocalAuthentication framework. We do not have access to your biometric data (facial maps or fingerprints); the authentication is handled entirely by the iOS system.
  • App PIN: If you set a custom 4-digit PIN, this code is stored locally in an encrypted format on your device. We cannot recover this PIN for you if it is forgotten.

3. Data Storage

Fibres does not maintain a central user database. All personal content, including photos, comparison data, and security settings, is stored locally on your device.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

Fibres offers subscriptions and digital purchases through Apple’s App Store. We do not collect or store your credit card or billing information. All transactions are handled securely by Apple via the RevenueCat framework for subscription management.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third-party services. Because your data is stored locally, we do not have access to your photos or personal usage habits.

6. Your Rights and Data Control

You have full control over your information:

  • Deletion: You can delete individual photos or use the "Purge All Data" feature in the Settings menu to wipe all local records instantly.
  • Uninstallation: Deleting the App from your device will permanently remove all locally stored photos and data. Because we do not use cloud backups, this data cannot be recovered once the App is deleted.

7. Children’s Privacy

Fibres is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
