Privacy Policy

App Name: Fibres - Progress Photos

Last Updated: January 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Fibres - Progress Photos (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles your information.

1. Photos and Images

All photos added to Fibres - Progress Photos remain on your device and within the app. We do not permanently store your photos on our own servers.

If you choose to use features that require photo analysis (available to premium users), selected photos are securely transmitted to a third-party AI service (OpenAI) solely for the purpose of analysis.

  • Photos are sent only when analysis is requested by the user
  • Photos are used strictly to generate analysis results
  • Photos are not stored by OpenAI after processing
  • Photos are not used to train AI models

2. AI Analysis Data

Text-based analysis results generated from your photos are stored locally on your device so you can view them within the app.

These analysis results remain accessible while your premium subscription is active. If you cancel your premium subscription, access to premium analysis features and stored analysis results may be removed.

3. Data Storage

Fibres - Progress Photos does not maintain a central user database containing your photos. All personal content, including images and analysis results, is stored locally on your device unless explicitly stated otherwise.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

Fibres - Progress Photos offers auto-renewable subscriptions through Apple’s App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

All payments and subscription management are handled by Apple. You can manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID account settings.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services only when necessary to provide core functionality, including AI-powered image analysis.

These services may temporarily process data solely to perform requested operations and are contractually restricted from using your data for other purposes.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers. Your information is only shared when required to provide app functionality or comply with legal obligations.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Data transmitted for analysis is sent over secure connections. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

Fibres - Progress Photos is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights

You control your data within the app. You may delete photos at any time by removing them from the app. Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the app’s support channels.
