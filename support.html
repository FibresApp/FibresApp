Fibres – Progress Photos

Thanks for using Fibres. If you have questions, need help, or are experiencing any issues with the app, we’re here to help.

Common Topics

  • Security & PIN Access
    If you have enabled Face ID or a custom App PIN and are having trouble unlocking the app, please note that for your privacy, we do not store these codes on our servers. If you are permanently locked out, you may need to reinstall the app (note: this will remove your locally stored photos).
  • Managing Photos
    Fibres is a "Local First" app. Your photos are stored safely on your device. If you are having trouble viewing or importing photos, ensure the app has permission to access your Photo Library in your iPhone Settings.
  • Subscription & Billing
    All subscriptions and payments are handled securely by Apple. For billing issues, payment disputes, or refund requests, please contact the Apple App Store directly as we do not have access to your financial records.
  • Data Privacy
    We do not see, store, or sell your photos. Everything stays on your device. For a detailed breakdown, please view our Privacy Policy in the app settings.
  • Bug Reports & Feedback
    We love hearing from you. If you find a bug or have a suggestion for a new feature, please send us an email with a description and any relevant screenshots.

Subscriptions & Billing

To manage your subscription, open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, and select Subscriptions. From there, you can view, change, or cancel your Fibres subscription.
