Privacy Policy

Fibres – Progress Photos

Last updated: January 9, 2026

Fibres – Progress Photos (“Fibres”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application available on iOS and Android.

1. Information We Collect

User-Provided Content

Fibres allows users to upload photos for the purpose of tracking fitness progress. These photos are uploaded voluntarily by the user and are used only to provide app functionality such as progress tracking and photo comparison.

If you choose to use AI-powered features, uploaded images may be processed by a third-party AI service to generate analysis and insights.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use user-provided information solely to:

  • Enable photo storage and progress comparison
  • Provide AI-based image analysis (if enabled)
  • Deliver app features and functionality
  • Manage subscriptions and access to premium features

We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. AI Image Analysis

Fibres uses a third-party AI service to analyze user-uploaded images when AI features are enabled. This analysis is performed only to provide feedback and insights within the app.

  • Images are not used to identify users
  • Images are not sold
  • Images are not used to train AI models for unrelated purposes

4. Subscriptions and Payments

Fibres uses RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. Payment information is handled directly by:

  • Apple App Store (iOS)
  • Google Play Store (Android)

Fibres does not collect or store payment or billing information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

Data may be shared only with service providers required to operate app features, such as AI processing and subscription management. These providers are required to handle data securely and only for the intended purpose.

6. Data Retention

User-uploaded photos and related data are retained as long as your account remains active or until you choose to delete them. You may delete your content at any time from within the app.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children’s Privacy

Fibres is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access or delete it. You can exercise these rights by contacting us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL, and the “Last updated” date will be revised.
